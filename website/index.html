The risks of the sun

Everywhere you go, the sun is there with you. And even though many of us like the beautiful sunlight on our divine bodies, it doesn't come without its risks.

There are several types of skin cancer which can be caused by exposing your skin to too much sunlight. Excessive exposure to sunlight is even considered to be the number 1 cause of skin cancer in general. The younger and longer you're exposed to sunlight, the higher the risk of developing skin cancer at an older age.

Sunlight damages the elastic fibers in your skin on the long term. It can make your skin dryer and more wrinkly.
